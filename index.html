Our Mission

The mission of the Asian-American Pacific Islander Club is to spread schoolwide awareness of the diverse history and culture of Asian backgrounds through a variety of presentations and activities promoting these ideas. You don't have to be Asian to join!

About

AAPI Club currently meets every other Monday. Please check the Google Classroom for announcements regarding the next meeting date. Each meeting covers a new aspect of Asian culture. Previous meetings themes have included: Squid Games, Bubble Tea, Sushi, Lunar New Year, and more!
